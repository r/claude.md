the decision we've been discussing).

Have it find the crux, name the load-bearing assumptions and how to test them, steelman the
alternative I'm leaning against, and attack the leading choice for second-order effects and the
cases it doesn't cover — plus any better option not yet on the table.

Relay its sharpened decision: the crux, the assumptions, the real risks ranked, and the cheapest
thing that would most reduce the uncertainty. It surfaces and stress-tests; the call stays mine.
Don't let it cheerlead or manufacture risk.
